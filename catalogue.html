Beneath the Surface exhibition

Robert Rauschenberg spent a lot of time around dancers and created performance art pieces. The objective for the packaging of Robert Rauschenberg was to portray a sense of performance with a subtle narrative. As the packaging is delivered folded over, the audience are invited to open it out and explore each of the sections.
