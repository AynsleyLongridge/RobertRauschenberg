UNTITLED (CARDBOARD)

A piece from the series Cardboards 1971-72. The Cardboards are wall sculptures made from found cardboard boxes that have been cut, stapled, bent, and combined by the artist but retain their original history through stains, dents, and tears, in addition to inherent colour and labelling.

Cardboard, tape, and rubber hose
52 1/2 x 107 x 11 inches
(133.4 x 271.8 x 27.9 cm)
